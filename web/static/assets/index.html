Podcasting by podcasters

We love podcasting! After we released our first episodes by manually copying files to servers, making sure our MP3 metadata matched the episode's description, updating our RSS feed, and setting up the Web site for each of the episodes, we realized there had to be a better way. Because the major services lacked all the features that we found necessary, we set out to make a great podcasting platform. And, it's coming soon for you to use, too.

Collaborate

Guests & Co-hosts

While many podcasts feature the voice of a single talented individual, we find that podcasts with multiple voices have higher ratings. We make this easy for you by allowing you to find other great voices with whom you can collaborate. Or, you can just invite your friends to join you on your next episode. Whatever you decide, you can easily grow your conversation with the world.

Analyze

Listening Patterns

It's vitally important that you get as much information about your audience as possible. We believe that you shouldn't have to shell out big money to get access to as much data as you can get from people accessing and downloading your podcasts. Every single podcast gets the same high-quality information availabe for review, visualization, and download. It's your audience, after all.

Earn

Share Success

Finding sponsors for your podcast can be an incredibly hard undertaking. That's why we're creating an easy-to-use marketplace to find, approach, and schedule messages for your sponsors. You can share your audience data to get just the right companies. Then, collaborate on scripts with your sponsor and provide the recorded announcement to them for review in advance of your recording session.

Hear

Record to the Cloud

To get the best quality audio during a recording session, we have found the best solution is to have an individual audio file for each person participating. Most people, though, don't have the time to get everything set up and working. Our solution is simple: have each voice recorded separately on our servers. Then, you can download each of the files for the best-sounding episodes!
